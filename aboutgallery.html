4. Audio Project

Title: A Hero vs a Villain

Semester/Year: Fall 2019

Software: Audacity

Description: I interpret both hero and villain in this audio project by using a voice changer app to get a more accurate voice of the characters.

A small village is being attacked by a villain, people screaming and running for their lives through destroyed buildings by explosions, and the villain`s malefic laugh. Moments later, a hero comes in to save the day. The hero is a tiny but powerful individual, never underestimate him! But that is what the villain did. It ended up being the worst mistake of his life, it cost him his life!

The hero tries to have a peaceful conversation with the villain to get to a non-violent conclusion, but the villain only wants to kill people. That must not be allow! And the hero does what he has to do.

5. Animation Project

Title: I Want That Carrot!!!

Semester/Year: Fall 2019

Software: Adobe After Effects 2018

Description: Everything (the shapes and effects) in this project is made from after effects, except for the rabbit. The rabbit is from the first project in Photoshop.

I use text and a wide variety of key frames and effects. Key frames, such as scale, position, opacity, etc. and effects like, radio wave for the sun and wave warp for water. In addition, the background is made with a gradient effect that has green for the ground and blue for the sky, which is changing gradually to give the impression of the dawn, day time, and night time.

This project is about a rabbit that sees a huge carrot and goes for it spending the entire day walking up and down in the mountains. He finally gets to the carrot at evening. The rabbit eats the gigantic carrot, which makes him huge afterward.
