HELLO EVERYBODY!

Animation made with PROCREATE

Title: Hello!

Software: Procreate

Description: This is a 2D animation created with the app PROCREATE.

It has 91 frames in total with a frame rate of 5 frames per second. Also, it is all hand-drawn

This animation is about a brush that is writing two Chinese characters. It Says "Hello" in Chinese. At the end, a pencil draws a stickman, who waves to the viewer.
